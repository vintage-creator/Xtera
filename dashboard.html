Help Center

Welcome to Xtera! Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Log In & Connect Your Wallet:

    Click Sign In at the top right, choose your wallet (Ethereum or Solana), and approve the connection in your wallet popup.

  2. Sign the Nonce:

    After connecting, you’ll be prompted to sign a random message (nonce). This proves ownership of your address—no private keys are ever shared.

  3. Enable Two‑Factor Authentication:

    You’ll be redirected to the 2FA setup page.

    1. ✔️ Install an authenticator app (e.g. Authy or Google Authenticator).
    2. ✔️ Scan the QR code displayed.
    3. ✔️ Enter the 6‑digit code from your app and click Verify & Enable.
    4. ✔️ Once successful, you’ll return to the home page.

  4. Complete Your First‑Time KYC:

    When you make your first buy or sell via our fiat on‑ramp partner Transak, you’ll be guided through a quick, seamless identity verification (KYC).

  5. Buy, Sell & Swap Crypto:

    Back on the home page, choose Buy Crypto, Sell Crypto, or Swap Crypto. Enter your amounts, confirm with 2FA, and execute your trade instantly.
